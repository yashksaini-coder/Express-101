Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a check-up take?

Basic check-ups typically take 1-2 hours, while comprehensive and executive check-ups may take 3-4 hours.

Do I need to fast before the check-up?

Yes, for most blood tests, 8-12 hours of fasting is required. Water is allowed.

When will I get my results?

Basic test results are available within 24 hours. Some specialized tests may take 2-3 days.
